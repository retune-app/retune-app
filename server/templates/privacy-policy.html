Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

Welcome to Within. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our app. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Our Promise: We collect only what's needed to power your experience: account info, affirmations, voice samples, and usage stats. We never sell your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Name (optional, or provided through social login)
  • Authentication tokens for secure access

Voice Samples

If you choose to use our voice cloning feature, we collect:

  • Audio recordings you provide for voice cloning
  • The cloned voice model created from your sample

Your voice samples are processed through ElevenLabs, our trusted voice synthesis partner, solely for the purpose of creating your personalized affirmation audio.

Affirmations and Content

  • Goals and intentions you enter
  • AI-generated affirmation scripts
  • Custom categories you create

Usage Data

  • Listening session duration and frequency
  • Breathing exercise sessions
  • Feature usage patterns
  • App performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and personalize your affirmation experience
  • Generate AI-powered affirmation scripts tailored to your goals
  • Create your personal voice clone for audio playback
  • Track your progress and streaks
  • Improve our app and services
  • Send you notifications (if enabled)
  • Respond to support requests

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI: For generating personalized affirmation scripts
  • ElevenLabs: For voice cloning and text-to-speech synthesis
  • Apple Sign In / Google Sign In: For secure authentication

Each service has its own privacy policy and data handling practices. We only share the minimum data necessary for these services to function.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure password hashing
  • Token-based authentication
  • Regular security updates

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account:

  • Your personal data is permanently deleted
  • Voice samples and cloned voices are removed
  • Affirmations and listening history are erased

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Portability: Export your affirmations and data

To exercise these rights, use the settings within the app or contact us directly.

7. Children's Privacy

Within is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or via email.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us:
