Frequently Asked Questions

How do I record my voice sample?

Tap the microphone icon on the home screen and read the provided script clearly. This creates your personalized voice clone for affirmations.

How do I create a new affirmation?

Tap the gold "+" button at the bottom of the screen. Enter your goal, select a life pillar, and our AI will generate personalized affirmations for you.

What are the breathing techniques?

We offer Box Breathing (4-4-4-4), 4-7-8 Relaxation, and Coherent Breathing. Each technique is designed to help you relax and focus.

How do I mark affirmations as favorites?

Tap the heart icon on any affirmation card to add it to your favorites. Use the filter button to view only your favorites.

What is RSVP mode?

Rapid Serial Visual Presentation displays affirmation words one at a time in sync with audio, creating a deep focus experience.

Privacy & Data

Your privacy is important to us. Voice samples and affirmations are stored securely and never shared with third parties. You can delete your data at any time from the Settings menu.
